About Us

Empowering Namibia with reliable economic insights.

The Namibia Economic Observatory was founded by Michael Ndiiwda Henok, an upcoming economist with experience gained from the University of Namibia and international internship exposure. The Observatory transforms complex economic data into clear insights, dashboards and visual intelligence for researchers, students, businesses, policy makers and the general public. Our mission is to provide accessible, reliable and data-driven economic information that supports better decision-making in Namibia.

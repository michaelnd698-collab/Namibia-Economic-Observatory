About Us

The Namibia Economic Observatory was founded by Michael Ndiiwda Henok, an upcoming economist with experience and knowledge gained from the University of Namibia and international internship exposure. The platform aims to bring market research, data analytics and visualization to Namibian economic information users by transforming complex economic data into understandable insights and interactive visual intelligence. Our mission is to provide accessible, data-driven economic insights for Namibia through research, analytics and visualization.
